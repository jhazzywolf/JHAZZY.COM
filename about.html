THE WOLF

Born and raised in Denver, Colorado, Jhazzy Wolf is a multi-genre independent artist bringing a much-needed feminine energy to the industry.

Preaching individuality, strength, and self-love; it is serendipitous for a woman who calls herself a wolf to be the one cutting through. She builds community and elevates the scene, consistently proving that her artistry is built on authentic connections and undeniable talent.
